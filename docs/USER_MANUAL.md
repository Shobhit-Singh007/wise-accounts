# Wise Accounts - User Manual

## Getting Started

### Installation
1. Download from Google Play Store (Android) or App Store (iOS)
2. Open the app and tap "Register"
3. Enter your phone number and create a password
4. Verify OTP (if enabled)
5. Create your business profile with GSTIN

### First Time Setup
1. Add your business details (name, GSTIN, address)
2. Set up your UPI ID for receiving payments
3. Add your first products/services
4. Add customers

---

## Dashboard
The dashboard shows:
- **Today's Sales** — Total sales for today
- **Pending Amount** — Outstanding payments from customers
- **Total Customers** — Number of registered customers
- **Total Products** — Number of products/services
- **Recent Invoices** — Last 5 invoices created
- **Sales Chart** — 7-day sales trend

---

## Customers

### Adding a Customer
1. Go to **Customers** → **Add Customer**
2. Enter name, phone, email (optional)
3. Set credit limit (optional)
4. Save

### Customer Ledger
- View all transactions with a customer
- See opening balance, invoices, payments, closing balance
- Export ledger as PDF

### Customer Groups
- Create groups (e.g., "Wholesale", "Retail", "VIP")
- Assign customers to groups for easy filtering

---

## Products & Inventory

### Adding a Product
1. Go to **Products** → **Add Product**
2. Enter name, HSN code, price
3. Set GST rate (0%, 5%, 12%, 18%, 28%)
4. Set stock quantity and low-stock threshold
5. Save

### Stock Management
- **Stock Adjustment** — Manual stock corrections
- **Stock Transfer** — Move stock between warehouses
- **Low Stock Alerts** — Notifications when stock is low
- **Batch/Expiry Tracking** — Track batches with expiry dates

### Barcode Scanning
1. Go to **Products** → **Scan Barcode**
2. Point camera at barcode
3. Product is automatically looked up

---

## Billing & Invoices

### Creating an Invoice
1. Go to **Invoices** → **Create Invoice**
2. Select customer
3. Add products (search or scan barcode)
4. GST is auto-calculated (CGST+SGST for intra-state, IGST for inter-state)
5. Review and save

### Invoice Types
- **B2B** — Business to Business (with GSTIN)
- **B2C** — Business to Consumer (without GSTIN)

### Invoice Actions
- **Share** — Send via WhatsApp, Email, SMS
- **Download PDF** — Save invoice as PDF
- **Print** — Print via Bluetooth thermal printer or AirPrint
- **E-Way Bill** — Generate e-way bill for goods >₹50,000
- **e-Invoice** — Generate IRN for B2B invoices

### Bulk Invoices
1. Go to **Invoices** → **Bulk Invoice**
2. Select multiple customers
3. Add common items
4. Generate all invoices at once

---

## Payments

### Recording a Payment
1. Go to **Payments** → **Record Payment**
2. Select customer and invoice
3. Enter amount paid
4. Choose payment method (Cash, UPI, Bank Transfer, Card, Razorpay, Cheque)
5. Save

### UPI Payment
1. Go to **Payments** → **UPI Payment**
2. Enter amount and description
3. Generate QR code or UPI link
4. Customer scans and pays

### Razorpay Payment
1. Go to **Payments** → **Razorpay Payment**
2. Enter amount
3. Tap "Pay" — customer completes payment in Razorpay checkout
4. Payment is auto-recorded on success

### Payment Reconciliation
- **Manual** — Match payments to invoices manually
- **Auto** — System matches payments by amount automatically

### Payment Reminders
1. Go to **Payments** → **Reminders**
2. View overdue invoices
3. Send individual or bulk reminders via SMS/Email

## Reports

### Available Reports
- **Sales Report** — Daily, weekly, monthly, yearly sales
- **GST Report** — GSTR-1, GSTR-3B summary
- **HSN Report** — HSN-wise sales breakdown
- **Customer Report** — Customer-wise sales and outstanding
- **Product Report** — Product-wise sales and stock
- **Profit & Loss** — Revenue, expenses, net profit
- **Inventory Valuation** — Current stock value
- **Payment Collection** — Payments received
- **Outstanding Report** — Pending payments

### Exporting Reports
- Export any report as **PDF** or **CSV**
- Share via WhatsApp, Email, or save to device

## Staff Management

### Inviting Staff
1. Go to **Settings** → **Staff** → **Invite**
2. Enter staff phone number
3. Select role (Owner, Admin, Manager, Accountant, Sales, Viewer)
4. Staff receives invitation notification

### Permissions
Each role has specific permissions:
- **Owner** — Full access
- **Admin** — Most access except billing settings
- **Manager** — Customer, product, invoice management
- **Accountant** — Payments, reports, reconciliation
- **Sales** — Customers, invoices, payments
- **Viewer** — Read-only access

---

## Settings

### Business Settings
- Business name, GSTIN, address
- Bank details for NEFT/RTGS
- UPI ID for QR payments
- Invoice prefix and numbering

### Invoice Settings
- Default invoice type (B2B/B2C)
- Default payment terms
- Invoice template selection
- Terms and conditions

### App Settings
- Language (English / हिन्दी)
- Dark Mode (follows system or manual toggle)
- Notification preferences

## Offline Mode
The app works offline:
- Create invoices, record payments, manage customers/products
- Data syncs automatically when connection is restored
- Sync status indicator shows connection state
- Conflict resolution handles concurrent edits
